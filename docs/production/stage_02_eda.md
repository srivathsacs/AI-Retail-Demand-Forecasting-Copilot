# Stage 2 - Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## Objective

Understand the retail dataset before feature engineering and forecasting.

---

## Purpose

EDA was performed only during experimentation to:

- Understand the data.
- Identify quality issues.
- Discover trends and seasonality.
- Validate assumptions.

EDA is **not part of the production pipeline**.

---

## Key Findings

- Sales exhibit strong seasonality.
- Promotions significantly impact demand.
- Missing dates are minimal.
- Product categories behave differently.
- Forecasting should be performed per store and category.

---

## Production Decision

No production code was created.

The insights from EDA informed the design of later stages.
